# AMPSphere

<a href="https://ampsphere.big-data-biology.org/"><img src="/images/websites/screenshot_ampshere.png" alt="AMPSphere screenshot" style="max-width: 50%" /></a>

[AMPSphere](https://ampsphere.big-data-biology.org/) is a comprehensive catalog of 863,498 non-redundant antimicrobial peptides (AMPs) predicted from 63,410 metagenomes and 87,920 microbial genomes using machine learning. It enables exploration of AMP diversity across habitats and provides insights into the evolutionary origins of these peptides.

See: [Discovery of antimicrobial peptides in the global microbiome with machine learning](/paper/2024_ampsphere).

# GMSC & GMSC-mapper

<a href="https://gmsc.big-data-biology.org/"><img src="/images/websites/screenshot_gmsc.png" alt="GMSC screenshot" style="max-width: 50%" /></a>

The [Global Microbial smORF Catalogue](https://gmsc.big-data-biology.org/) (GMSC) contains 965 million non-redundant small open reading frames (smORFs) derived from 63,410 metagenomes across 75 habitats and 87,920 isolate genomes, with comprehensive annotations. A companion tool, [GMSC-mapper](https://github.com/BigDataBiology/GMSC-mapper), is available to identify and annotate small proteins from microbial (meta)genomes.

See: [A catalog of small proteins from the global microbiome](/paper/2024_GMSC).

# Shanghai Dog MAGs

<a href="https://sh-dog-mags.big-data-biology.org/"><img src="/images/websites/screenshot_sh-dogs.png" alt="Shanghai Dog MAGs screenshot" style="max-width: 50%" /></a>

The [Shanghai Dog MAGs viewer](https://sh-dog-mags.big-data-biology.org/) is a companion website for our study on pet dog gut microbiomes using long-read metagenomics, providing access to 2,676 MAGs representing 320 bacterial species from 51 urban pet dogs in Shanghai.

See: [Capturing global pet dog gut microbial diversity and hundreds of near-finished bacterial genomes by using long-read metagenomics in a Shanghai cohort](/paper/2025_sh_dogs).

# Urban Soil MAGs

<a href="https://urban-soil-mags.big-data-biology.org/"><img src="/images/websites/screenshot_urban-soil.png" alt="Urban Soil MAGs screenshot" style="max-width: 50%" /></a>

The [Urban Soil MAGs viewer](https://urban-soil-mags.big-data-biology.org/) is a companion website for our study on urban soil microbiomes, providing access to 7,949 medium- and high-quality MAGs from 58 urban soil samples.

See: [Long-read metagenomic sequencing reveals novel lineages and functional diversity in urban soil microbiome](/paper/2026_urban_soil_longread).

# Web Bio Tools

<a href="https://web-bio-tools.big-data-biology.org/"><img src="/images/websites/screenshot_web-bio-tools.png" alt="Web Bio Tools screenshot" style="max-width: 50%" /></a>

[Web Bio Tools](https://web-bio-tools.big-data-biology.org/) is a collection of simple bioinformatics tools compiled to WebAssembly, enabling sequence alignment and other analyses directly in the browser.

# Macrel

[Macrel](/software/macrel/) (for metagenomic AMP classification and retrieval)
is an end-to-end pipeline for the prospection of high-quality AMP candidates
from (meta)genomes. It is available for download or online use.
